About US

We Are Their Voice

It doesn’t take much to know that, just like us, animals too are living beings that feel pain, feel neglected, and if need be, require saving. Also, if you’ve ever owned a pet, you’ll agree that the bond one shares with animals is a different emotion altogether, one that can’t be summed up in words. However, unlike our pets, a lot of animals out there aren’t lucky enough to have a loving home, with many of them living in incredibly dangerous and hazardous situations. Our goal at Scooby's Shelter is to make shelters the first place potential adopters turn when looking to get a new pet, ensuring that all healthy and treatable pets find loving homes. We do this by breaking down misconceptions surrounding shelter pets and celebrating the unique bond between every shelter pet and parent.
